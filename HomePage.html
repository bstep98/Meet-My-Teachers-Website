Welcome!

Sophomore year has begun! I am no longer a freshman, and I am no longer living on campus! Getting to campus every morning is a bit of a hassle, but it's really worth it when you take classes as fun as I do! This semester, I'm currently enrolled in 5 courses. On Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays I go to Intro to Creative and Writing and Intro to Theatre. I also go to Visual Media on those same days, except Fridays. On Tuesdays and Thursdays, I go to Media and Society and Intro to Acting. My schedule definitely has a nice balance between work and fun, and that's exactly the way I like it! It's a lot of work, but I am more than willing to put in the effort for these classes.Feel free to take a deeper look into the specific pages for each of these classes, where I go into more depth into the courses themselves and the professors that teach them!
